Why Sleep Cycles Matter

Most adults need between 7 to 9 hours of sleep per night, but it’s not just about the total hours — it’s about sleep cycles. Each cycle lasts about 90 minutes, and waking up in the middle of a cycle can leave you feeling groggy. Our Sleep Schedule Optimizer helps you wake up between cycles, so you start your day feeling refreshed.

Tips for Better Sleep

  • Go to bed at the same time every night.
  • Avoid caffeine and heavy meals before bedtime.
  • Keep your bedroom cool, dark, and quiet.
  • Limit screen time at least 1 hour before sleep.
  • Consider light stretching or meditation before bed.

FAQ: Common Sleep Questions

1. Is 6 hours of sleep enough?

While some people function on 6 hours, most adults need 7–9 hours for optimal health and productivity.

2. Can naps replace lost sleep?

Naps can improve alertness, but they don’t fully replace the benefits of a full night’s sleep.

3. What’s the best time to wake up?

There’s no universal best time — it depends on your lifestyle and work schedule. Our calculator helps you find your personalized time.
