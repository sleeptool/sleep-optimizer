Quick Tips for Better Sleep

  • Keep a consistent sleep and wake schedule (even weekends).
  • Aim for 4–6 full cycles (6–9 hours) when possible.
  • Limit caffeine after mid-afternoon and keep your room cool, dark, and quiet.
  • Use a short power nap (10–25 min) when you’re dragging — avoid long evening naps.

These tips are general info, not medical advice.

FAQ

What is a sleep cycle and why 90 minutes?

Sleep cycles average ~90 minutes across light, deep, and REM stages. Waking at the end of a cycle tends to feel easier.

How many cycles should I aim for?

Common targets are 4–6 cycles (≈6–9 hours). Life happens — use the planner to get the best you can.

Are these tools medical advice?

No — they’re general educational tools. If you have ongoing sleep issues, consult a medical professional.

Do you store my times or personal data?

No. The tools calculate entirely in your browser. See Privacy for details on ads/analytics cookies.
